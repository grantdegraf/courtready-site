About CourtReady

CourtReady LLC is a specialized drafting and research support service that works alongside licensed attorneys. We provide structured document preparation, authority research, and organizational support for litigation matters.

Our role is to handle the methodical, time-intensive aspects of litigation document work so that counsel can direct their attention to legal strategy, client advocacy, and courtroom preparation.

CourtReady is not a law firm. We do not provide legal advice, appear in court, or establish attorney-client relationships. All work product is prepared for review, approval, and use by licensed counsel.

Scope and Disclaimers

CourtReady LLC provides document preparation and research support services only. Our services do not constitute legal advice, and use of our services does not create an attorney-client relationship.

CourtReady does not represent clients in court proceedings, make legal determinations, or guarantee any particular outcome. All work product is subject to review, modification, and approval by licensed counsel.

Counsel is solely responsible for all final legal decisions, jurisdiction-specific compliance, and the substantive content of any document filed with a court.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you turn around a motion?

Standard turnaround for motion papers is 48 hours from scope confirmation. Expedited timelines are available for urgent filings. Appellate briefs typically require 7-10 business days depending on complexity.

What does the initial review include?

We review the decision or order, identify available procedural paths, and provide a short case-specific outline of the strongest available ground for further action. There is no charge for this initial assessment.

How do you handle confidentiality?

All client materials are handled under strict confidentiality protocols. We execute NDAs on request and do not retain client files after engagement completion.

What are your rates?

Engagements are scoped on a flat-fee or per-project basis depending on the document type and complexity. Contact us for a quote — we provide pricing within 24 hours of intake.

Are revisions included?

Yes. Two rounds of revisions are included in every engagement at no additional cost.
