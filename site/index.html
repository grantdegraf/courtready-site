How It Works

01

Initial Consultation

Counsel submits case details including court, index number, key issues, and relevant deadlines. CourtReady assesses scope and confirms engagement terms.

02

Issue Analysis

CourtReady conducts structured research and issue framing, identifying applicable authority and preparing an organized analysis for attorney review.

03

Document Preparation

Draft outlines, formatted document shells, or complete research memoranda are prepared according to agreed scope and delivered to counsel.

04

Attorney Review & Finalization

All materials are reviewed, finalized, and confirmed by licensed counsel prior to submission. CourtReady does not file or submit documents independently.

About CourtReady

CourtReady LLC provides structured document preparation and legal research support services to licensed attorneys handling appellate matters.

We operate as a support function for counsel—not a substitute for it. Our work is designed to reduce administrative burden and accelerate the preparation phase of appellate proceedings, while keeping all legal determinations firmly in the hands of the attorney of record.

CourtReady is deadline-aware, process-driven, and organized around what courts and counsel actually need: accurate research, clean documents, and reliable delivery.
